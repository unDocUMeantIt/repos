koRpus.lang.ru: Language Support for 'koRpus' Package: Russian

Adds support for the Russian language to the 'koRpus' package. To ask for help, report bugs, suggest feature improvements, or discuss the global development of the package, please consider subscribing to the koRpus-dev mailing list (< https://korpusml.reaktanz.de >).
